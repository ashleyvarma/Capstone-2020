The Features

Your Own Guide

Simply select the floor and room you're in and see an interactive map of all the art around you.

Interact With Art

Click on any art piece in the interactive map to get additional information that often isn't accessible in museums.

Save Paintings

Maintain a list of pieces you want to look at later in your Art Passport.

Share With Friends

Find something cool? Quickly share information about pieces you've seen with your friends.
